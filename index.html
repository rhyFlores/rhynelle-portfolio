My Story

Hey there! I’m Rhynelle Kate, but most people call me Kate. I’m currently pursuing my degree in Information Technology at La Salle University – Ozamiz, and my curiosity for technology started with my love for games. That interest slowly grew into a passion for creating and designing websites that combine creativity and logic. I’m still learning, exploring new skills, and discovering my place in the tech world, but my goal is to keep growing while building meaningful and creative digital experiences.

When I’m not studying or coding, I enjoy watching movies or anime, playing games, scrabble, or badminton, and exploring new design ideas or tutorials. I love brainstorming projects and finding fun ways to express my ideas — because for me, the best work comes from doing what you love, learning from every step, and being surrounded by people who inspire you.
